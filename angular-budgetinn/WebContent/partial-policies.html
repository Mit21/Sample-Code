Special Request

➣ You must show a valid photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all special requests cannot be guaranteed and are subject to availability upon check-in.Additional charges may apply.

➣ Guests under 21 years old can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

➣ Cleaning services are based upon the number of nights stayed.

➣ For a stay of 8 nights or longer, the provided every other day cleaning service includes dusting, vacuuming, emptying the trash, cleaning the bathroom and more.

➣ Towels and linens are available at the front reception.

Children and Extra Beds.

➣ All children are welcome.

➣ Free! Up to two children under 15 years stay free of charge when using existing beds.

➣ Any additional older children or adults are charged USD 10 per person per night when using existing beds.

➣ All older children or adults are charged USD 10 per person per night for extra beds.

➣ Cribs are not available.

➣ The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.

➣ Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

➣ Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
