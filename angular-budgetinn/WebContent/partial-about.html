About Us

Property Location

Just off Highway 104 in Ontario, New York, this motel is a 20 minute drive from central Rochester and Greater Rochester International Airport (ROC)- 25 Min

Amenities

The Budget Inn Ontario features rooms with a microwave and a refrigerator. Simply decorated in warm colors and carpeted floors, the rooms also have a desk and a private bathroom.

At the Ontario Budget Inn, 24-hour front desk services are available. Free parking is also available.

Near By

A variety of dining options, including Salvatore’s Old Fashioned Pizzeria and Charlie Riedel’s Fast Food are less than one mile from the motel.

The Ontario Golf Course is a 10 minute drive from the motel. Seabreeze Amusement Park is 14 miles away.
